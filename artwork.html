On Screens Or Paper

I'm not much of an artist. I did marker art in 2017-2019 while spending my web life in the My Little Pony fandom, and had a brief digital stint in 2024. It was not the passion I would pursue myself, but I learned to appreciate the passion that goes into making artwork, and enjoy many artists, both those I am friends with, and those I browse for online. Here, you can find the artwork I have commissioned or received.

Characters and Personality

I started off with a dragon, Eve, in 2017. My OCs have since grown to several dozen, across many species. However, I still keep my roots dear, and Luna is still my representative fursona, whom I designed as the best version of who I want to become. Including them, here are some of the characters I own and love so dearly, from various eras.
