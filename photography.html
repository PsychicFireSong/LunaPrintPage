My life with photography

I've been taking pictures since 2014, starting on a humble Instax mini camera which i used to capture memories for keepsake while on vacations. Today, i shoot subjects from birds, to landscapes, to our fluffy friends (in both senses of the word) anywhere i can see them. I aim to capture the decisive moment, and interesting subjects, when I'm out and about, and go for clinical precision when shooting in the studio. You can find my work here.
