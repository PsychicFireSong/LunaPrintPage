Material Comparison

Disclaimer

Due to lighting setup inconsistency, any non-matte materials will have a colour shift on either side of the image. This is due to glare caused by the setup, and will be addressed in a future iteration of the system.

Methodology

These are comparisons of each material type, under normalized conditions. The images use the ARGB colour space and are preset to match the Nikon Z8-Neutral profile, the prints are lit using two 300W 95CRI LED studio lights with 60x90 softboxes, set to a temperature of 5600K. White balance is adjusted in DXO PhotoLab 8, setting each image to 5600K with a tint value of -6.
